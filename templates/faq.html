FAQs

If you have concerns regarding specific purchases, errors, or questions not listed, please contact princetonrebook@gmail.com.

I'm a Buyer


How can I search for books?
You can search for books by navigating to the search tab. You can search for books by title, ISBN number, author, or course name (e.g. COS126). Using our filters panel under the search bar, you can also sort your search results by date the listing was posted or prices, as well as filter by condition and price!
What is my Buyer Bookbag?
Your Buyer Bookbag is your personalized space for all the books you are interested in buying, are in the process of buying, or have previously bought in the past. In your 'In my bookbag' tab, you can see all the books you added to your bookbag. In your 'Pending' tab, you can see all the books you've paid for or have requested to buy. In your 'Completed' tab, you can view your purchase history for all previously purchased books. You can remove items from your bookbag, cancel transactions, and delete items from your purchase history here.
How do I save books I'm interested in for later?
You can save books to your Buyer Bookbag by clicking on the "Add to Buyer Bookbag" button on the listing page for any book that is not your own. Think of this as a bookmarks page where you can look over them later. This is not a shopping cart. Books in your bookbag will have a red bookmark icon when they appear in your search.
Why did some listings from my Bookbag disappear?
Sometimes, listings will disappear from your bookbag. This may be because the seller deleted the listing, or because someone moved forward with purchasing that listing. You should receive a red alert on the top of your Buyer Bookbag page the next time you refresh it or visit it, indicating which listings were affected.
How do I buy a book?

You can click the "Buy Now" button on the listing page for the book you would like to buy. It is up to you and the seller, whose contact information is provided to you, to carry out the delivery! Once you receive the book from the seller, please remind the seller to select the green checkmark button in their 'Pending' tab in the Buyer Bookbag to update the listing status.



I'm a Seller


What is my Seller Station?
Your Seller Station is your personalized space for all the books you are offering to sell, are in the process of selling, or have previously sold in the past. In your 'Active' tab, you can see all the books you are actively selling and can be found via search. In your 'Pending' tab, you can see all the books that someone is looking to pay for. In your 'Completed' tab, you can view your selling history for all previously sold books.
How do I post a listing?
You can post a listing via your Seller Station. Click the green "+ Post Listing" button to get started. Search for your book by ISBN to automatically retreive information regarding your book's publication information! Then, fill in the rest of the form indicating your book's condition, price, and course. You can also optionally add a description and images of your book. You may also edit the listing after it is posted.
What if my book is not available through the ISBN search?
Currently, rebook supports all books that are searchable on the Google Bookstore. Thus, rebook supports the vast majority of textbooks. However, there are specific rare instances where the ISBN database we are using does not have certain books, such as looseleaf (unbound) textbooks. Unfortunately, our platform does not currently support these rare exceptions. Please email us at princetonrebook@gmail.com to let us know about these.
How do I receive my payment?
When someone indicates that they will buy your book, you will receive an email notification about who the buyer is. In your Seller Station's 'Pending' tab, you can see the buyer's contact information. You must contact the buyer and discuss the logistics. Transactions are typically carried through cash or Venmo.
Can I deny someone's request to buy my book?
You may deny someone's request to buy your book via your Seller Station's 'Pending' tab. You may do this for several reasons: for example, if the information on your listing (e.g. price, description, etc) was inaccurate, or if the buyer's payment logistics is inconvenient for you. The listing will then be returned to the search page. However, rebook does not tolerate nor condone any discrimination. Denying a purchase request based on the buyer's identity is against our terms of service.
